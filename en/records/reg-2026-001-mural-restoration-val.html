Activity record · REG-2026-001

Corrective mural restoration in Villa Alberto Larraguibel.

First territorial action documented by N.A.H.U.E.L. Foundation: a heritage maintenance and visual reintegration intervention carried out on February 17, 2026, from 18:30 to 20:00, in La Florida, Chile.

Villa Alberto Larraguibel · Community mural 2024

Restoring the visual reading of a community artwork.

The activity consisted of neutralizing a localized act of vandalism and chromatically reintegrating the affected area through controlled repainting. The work protected the visual continuity of the mural and its cultural value for the everyday environment of the resident community.

The procedure included visual inspection, delimitation of the affected area, initial photographic record, preparation of compatible tones, controlled reintegration, final review and closing documentation.
